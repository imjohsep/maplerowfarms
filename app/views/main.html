About

Maple Row Farms is a family owned and operated agricultural business located in Northern New Jersey. It has been in opperation for nearly two centuries raising dairy cattle, growing corn, alfalfa, and wheat. To address the new demands of the market a herd of beef cattle, soy beans, and organic garlic have been added to the harvest.
